Hi, my name is Harrison Anthony and welcome to my website. Here you will find information about my current research projects. I just recently finished my PhD at the 
University of Galway where I studied microsatellite instability and the tools used to detect it in sequencing data. I am now a postdoctoral researcher at the Princess Máxima Center, where I focus on 
better understanding the genetic causes of CAR-T relapse in pediatric cancer. I general, my research interests lie in cancer 
evolution, tumor heterogeneity, and biomarker detection. These themes are present throughout my current
research and the chapters of my PhD thesis.

I am always open to collaboration and feedback on my research. Please use the website links 
to my social media or email if you would like to get into contact.
